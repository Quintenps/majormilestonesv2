What is SAFe®?
What is Scaled Agile?

Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) is a set of best-practises used to manage and execute large-scale software development projects, particularly in an enterprise environment. It is based on the principles of Lean-DevSecOps and Agile software development and is designed to be flexible and scalable for organizations of different sizes and industries.  

Why SAFe®?

SAFe® is intended to help teams work together more efficiently and effectively, and to deliver software faster and with higher quality. It includes principles, practices, and roles for managing and coordinating work across multiple teams, and it provides a framework for planning, tracking, and delivering software releases. 

Benefits of SAFe®

The Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) can provide therefore also several benefits for you including: 

  1. Faster delivery of software: SAFe helps teams work together more efficiently and effectively, which can lead to faster delivery of software. 
  2. Increased collaboration: SAFe encourages collaboration across teams and departments, which can help to improve communication and coordination.
  3. Improved quality: SAFe includes practices for managing and testing software, which can help to improve the quality of the software being delivered.
  4. Better alignment with business goals: SAFe helps organizations align their software development efforts with their overall business goals and objectives.
  5. Greater visibility: SAFe provides a framework for planning, tracking, and delivering software releases, which can help organizations to better understand the status of their software development efforts and identify potential issues early on.
  6. Better scalability: SAFe is designed to be flexible and scalable, which can help organizations to better manage large-scale software development projects and adapt to changing business needs.
