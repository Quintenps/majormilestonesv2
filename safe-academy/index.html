Find the right course for you 

Choosing the right course can be a daunting task, with so many options available it can be hard to know where to start. The key to finding the right course for you is to identify your goals and what you hope to achieve from the training. Within SAFe there are 6 options: 

  1. Foundation courses
  2. Process oriented courses
  3. Content oriented courses
  4. Technology oriented courses
  5. Transformation oriented courses
  6. Branche specific courses 

Once you have a clear idea of what you want to achieve, you can start researching the different courses that are available and determine which one will best help you reach your goals.
