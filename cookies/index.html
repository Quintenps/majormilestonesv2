Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how our Portal uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and collect information about your usage of the Portal. By continuing to browse or use our Portal, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit certain websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide information to the website owners.

How we use cookies
We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of our Portal and enable you to navigate and use its features. Without these cookies, certain functionalities may be unavailable.

Analytical cookies: We use analytical cookies to collect information about how visitors use our Portal. This helps us analyze and improve the performance and functionality of our Portal. The information collected is anonymized and does not identify individual users.

Functional cookies: These cookies allow our Portal to remember choices you make and provide enhanced features and personalization. They may be set by us or third-party providers whose services we have added to our Portal.

Advertising cookies: We may use advertising cookies to deliver relevant advertisements to you based on your interests. These cookies track your browsing habits and enable us to tailor advertising content to your preferences.

Third-party cookies: Our Portal may include content and services provided by third parties. These third parties may set cookies on your device while you interact with their content, such as embedded videos or social media sharing buttons. We have no control over these cookies and their usage. Please refer to the respective third party's privacy and cookies policies for more information.

Managing cookies
You have the option to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of our Portal.

You can usually manage cookie settings through your browser's settings or options. The "Help" function within your browser should provide you with the necessary instructions.

For more information about managing cookies, please visit www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/.

Changes to our Cookies Policy
We may update our Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top. We encourage you to review this Cookies Policy periodically for any updates or changes.
